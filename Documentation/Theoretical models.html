Theoretical models

Each group of datapoints can be refined with its own combination of theoretical models. For detailed description of the models see the Theoretical models section. If you want to fit the datapoints corresponding to the individual groups, the Fit groups tickbox has to be ticked. Check, if you have all mandatory information for the refinement according to the model (e.g. material constants, or azimuthal φ angles). If a problem would arise during the refinement, the program will produce warning or error dialogs to suggest what is probably wrong.

If the refinement is successfuly finished, the results are plotted in the graphs on the left side of the main window and printed as a text and numbers inside the Result section (also printed in the Matlab console). The figures and results can be then saved by the Save figures and Save results buttons respectively.
